Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need priot programming or cybersecurity experience to join CSC?

No experience is required! We welcome all beginners and experience students, and all our activities are designed to be beginner-friendly.

Who can join the Cybersecurity Club (CSC)?

All NYU Shanghai students are welcome to join, regardless of major or academic background/interests.

How often does the club meet?

The club usually meets on weekly Mondays at 7pm on campus.

How can I join CSC?

You can join by submitting your application, attending weekly meetings, and joining our Wechat groupchat for updates. All interested students are welcome!

Are there leadership positions and/or opportunities?

Yes. You can apply to become a general member or E-board member. You are welcome to take on active roles for cybersecurity event organization, marketing, etc.
