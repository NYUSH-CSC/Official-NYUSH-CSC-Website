About Us

The NYUSH Cybersecurity Club is a student-led community passionate about digital security, privacy, and ethical hacking. We bring together curious minds to explore the fascinating world of cybersecurity through hands-on learning and collaboration. Whether you're a complete beginner or have experience in security, our club provides a supportive environment to develop practical skills, participate in competitions, and connect with peers who share your interests.

What We Do

  • Hands-on workshops & tutorials
  • Participate in Capture the Flag (CTF) competitions
  • Guest speakers share insights on the latest trends in cybersecurity
  • Build a community for networking and growth

Our Focus Areas

Capture The Flag

Compete in CTF competitions to solve security challenges and develop problem-solving skills.

Security Workshops

Learn through hands-on workshops covering penetration testing, cryptography, and more.

Networks & Systems

Explore network security, system administration, and infrastructure protection.

Ethical Hacking

Master defensive and offensive security techniques in a responsible, ethical framework.
